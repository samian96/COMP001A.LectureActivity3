Parakeets

Parakeets are a small species of parrots that originate from various regions of the world. Budgerigar, as show in this picture, originate from Australia being one of the most popular pet birds.

A cockatiel is the smallest parrot of the cockatoo family. They are widely loved for their unique feather pattern, as well as their vocal personality. Cockatiels are native to Australia and can co-exist with parakeets.

All types of vegetables and fruits are an important part of a birds diet to keep them healthy and strong. This is essential information to know if you plan on becoming a bird owner.
